Account Deletion Request – Clippings Scheduler by Chaseware

Users of the Clippings Scheduler may request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion:
Email us at codebybrepo+support@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account and include the subject line:
"Account Deletion Request"

Data that will be deleted:
• User account (authentication record)
• Profile information (name, phone number, address)
• Job history and service records
• Messages and notifications

Data that may be retained:
• Transaction or billing records (if required by law)
• Aggregated or anonymized analytics data

Retention period:
All personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of request.
